Good to know

Donor questions, answered

Is my general gift really tax-deductible?

Yes — general and endowment gifts to our 501(c)(3) are deductible. Zakat designations are deductible too as charitable gifts; their zakat validity is governed separately by how they're disbursed.

Can a single gift be both zakat and deductible?

The deduction is about your taxes; zakat validity is about use and recipient. We honor both by segregating zakat funds and disbursing them only as grants to eligible recipients.

What happens if a borrower can't repay?

If they're zakat-eligible, the zakat pocket forgives the balance and the lending pool is made whole — so your endowment gift stays intact.
