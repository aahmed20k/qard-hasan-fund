Reassurance

What applicants ask us

Will I be charged any interest or fees?

Never. Qard hasan means a benevolent, interest-free loan. You repay only what you borrowed, on a schedule you can manage.

What if I genuinely can't repay later?

If you fall into real hardship and qualify, the zakat arm can forgive your balance. The goal is relief, not pressure.

Is my information private?

Yes. Your details stay with the local review committee and are used only to assess and support your request.

Do I need perfect credit?

No. We lend on need and trust — references, character, and your community standing — not a credit score.
